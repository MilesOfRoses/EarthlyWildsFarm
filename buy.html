Buy Vegetables from Earthly Wilds Farm

In-person Farmers Market

Earthly Wilds Farm sells produce at the Elk Rapids farmers market every Friday morning from 8AM to 12PM from June 9th to October 6th. Located at 305 US-31, Elk Rapids, MI 49629


Online Pre-orders / Deliveries

Earthly wilds farm is a proud member of "Torch Lake Co-op", a collection of farmers in the Torch Lake area who sell our products collectively via the platform "Open Food Network". Here, you can pre-order your products and pick them up at the farmers market, or they can be delivered to you for a $7 charge if you live near the Traverse City area
